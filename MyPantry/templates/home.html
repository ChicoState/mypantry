Welcome to MyPantry!

Do you ever go to the store for a specific ingredient only to find that it was in the back of your pantry all along? Are there half-used ingredients in your fridge just waiting for the perfect recipe? Are you in a rut, bored of the same old things you make on a weekly basis? Do you have a hard time keeping track of new recipes you want to try? Having trouble pinning down what ingredients don't agree with you? With MyPantry, you can add ingredients that are currently existing in your pantry, browse our recommended recipes or add your own, create a grocery list, and keep a food diary. This all-in-one solution makes cooking a breeze by saving you trips to the grocery store, reducing your food waste and spending, spicing up your day-to-day with exciting new recipes, and even helping you keep track of possible food intolerance and allergies.
